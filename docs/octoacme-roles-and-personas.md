# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Facilitates agile ceremonies, removes blockers, and fosters team communication and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Coach team on agile best practices and continuous improvement
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- Remove impediments and escalate issues when needed
- Track and monitor action items from retrospectives
- Support team velocity and delivery momentum

### Goals
- Maximize team velocity and minimize blockers
- Foster psychological safety and team trust
- Drive continuous improvement through retrospective actions

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies (planning, review, retrospective)
- Action item tracker updates and retrospective notes
- Works with PM, PdM, Developers, and QA Lead to support daily execution

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Ensures a user-centric design approach throughout feature planning and delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Translate product requirements into wireframes and final UI/UX designs
- Partner with Product Manager on user research and usability testing
- Provide design system and component library guidance to developers
- Review implemented features for design fidelity and accessibility
- Advocate for consistent, accessible interfaces across the product

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce friction in product adoption
- Maintain a coherent design system across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with Developers and Product Manager
- Usability testing sessions and research findings reports
- Collaborates closely with Product Manager and Developers; consults with QA on usability

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Owns technical direction, ensures code quality, and mentors the development team.

### Responsibilities
- Guide developers on engineering best practices and architecture patterns
- Make key technical decisions and trade-offs on behalf of the project
- Review code for quality, testability, and maintainability
- Mentor team members and foster technical growth
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and technical debt
- Collaborate with QA Lead on testing strategy and quality gates

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable code
- Reduce technical debt and improve code quality over time
- Grow team technical skills and engineering culture

### Typical Communication
- Code review feedback and architecture decision records
- Technical design discussions with Developers and Product Manager
- Partners with Project Manager and Developers; advises Product Manager on technical trade-offs; works with QA Lead on quality approaches

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
Owns test strategy, coordinates testing efforts, and ensures quality gates are met before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define test plan and testing strategy for the project
- Author and review automated test scripts and test cases
- Lead defect triage, severity assessment, and reporting
- Partner with Developers on bug investigation and resolution
- Coordinate manual QA efforts and acceptance testing
- Track test coverage and quality metrics
- Validate that acceptance criteria are met before release

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and prevent production defects
- Enable fast feedback loops between development and testing
- Drive high test coverage and reliable quality metrics

### Typical Communication
- Defect triage meetings and quality status reports
- Test plans and test case documentation
- Collaborates with Project Manager, Developers, and Stakeholders; works closely with Technical Lead on testing strategy; validates acceptance criteria with Product Manager

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Bridges business objectives and technical delivery by translating stakeholder needs into clear, actionable requirements.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze requirements for feasibility and completeness
- Translate business needs into actionable user stories and acceptance criteria
- Facilitate requirements reviews with stakeholders and delivery team
- Support acceptance testing and validation
- Document business rules and regulatory requirements

### Goals
- Reduce misalignment between business and delivery
- Ensure requirements clarity and completeness before development begins
- Enable successful delivery through well-defined acceptance criteria

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- User stories, business rules documentation, and acceptance criteria
- Interfaces regularly with Product Manager and Stakeholders; supports Developers, QA, and Project Manager with requirement clarifications and validation
